What is NASI?

NASI stands for Naturing Adolescents, Youths, and Caregivers to Safeguard Integrity.

At Nasi, we understand the diverse and complex needs of adolescents, youths, and their caregivers. Through personalized counseling, therapy sessions, and support programs, we provide a safe space for adolescents and youths to explore and navigate their emotions, build resilience, and develop coping strategies for life's challenges. Additionally, we offer guidance and resources to caregivers, empowering them to effectively support the mental and emotional well-being of their loved ones. We're committed to empowering individuals and families to thrive. At Nasi, we're here to support you every step of the way, ensuring that no aspect of your well-being is overlooked. Our Services are both Online and Physical. NASI - Nurturing Adolescents, Youths, & Caregivers to Safeguard Integrity.
