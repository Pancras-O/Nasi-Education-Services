Annabel Kokeyo

Professional Mental Health Counselor at NASI Education Services

Professional Background

Annabel Kokeyo is a dedicated mental health professional and the founder of NASI Education Services, a mental health care organization based in Kisumu, Kenya. She established NASI in 2024 to provide personalized counseling, therapy, and support programs for adolescents, youths, and their caregivers. Her work focuses on mental health advocacy and education.

With a background in psychology, she leads NASI Education Services, ensuring individuals receive holistic mental health care. Her experience extends to working as a psychologist at Sunburst Projects Kenya since August 2023, where she provides counseling and therapy to vulnerable populations.

Academic Qualifications

Annabel is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Counseling Psychology at Great Lakes University. Her previous education includes a Diploma in Community Health and HIV Management at Thika School of Medical and Health Sciences. She is also certified by the Kenya Association of Professional Counselors and has completed training in Social and Behavioral Research.

Specializations and Approach

Annabel specializes in adolescent therapy, mental health awareness, psychosocial support, and education. Her approach is centered around providing safe spaces for individuals to explore emotional and psychological challenges. She offers counseling through one-on-one sessions, group therapy, and workshops.
