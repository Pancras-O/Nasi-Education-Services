Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) - NASI Education Services

Contact Us Learn More Our Services

NASI Education Services is a mental health care organization based in Kisumu, Kenya, providing counseling, therapy, and support programs for adolescents, youths, and caregivers. The organization offers both online and in-person services to help individuals navigate emotional and mental health challenges.

We offer a range of mental health services, including:
  • Individual Counseling
  • Group Therapy
  • Family Counseling
  • Online Counseling
  • Substance Abuse Therapy
  • Crisis Intervention & Trauma Therapy
  • Identity Exploration & LGBTQ Therapy
  • Holistic Student Support Program
  • Skill-Building Workshops

You can book a session by visiting our website and using the "Book a Session" feature, reaching out via our social media platforms, or contacting us through email or phone.
